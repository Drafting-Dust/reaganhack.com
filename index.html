04

About

"Design is the intersection of precision and atmosphere — where technical rigor gives form to human experience."

Reagan Hack is an Interior Architect and Designer based in Charlotte, NC, working across healthcare, commercial, and residential environments. With deep expertise in Revit BIM workflows, real-time visualization, and spatial programming, Reagan brings both technical precision and design vision to every project.

Beyond architecture, Reagan's work in art curation and data visualization bridges the gap between analytical thinking and visual storytelling — producing deliverables that communicate design intent clearly to clients, stakeholders, and collaborators.

Currently expanding into computational design, version-controlled project workflows, and interactive design graphics.
